SDG #3: GOOD HEALTH AND WELL-BEING

Health is a fundamental human right, yet many individuals lack access to essential healthcare services. This goal aims to reduce maternal and child mortality, combat communicable diseases, and promote mental health awareness. It advocates for universal access to quality healthcare services, ensuring that all individuals can lead healthy lives. Urdaneta City University prioritizes the health and well-being of its students by providing comprehensive mental health services, including counseling and wellness programs. The University organizes health awareness campaigns and workshops that promote physical fitness and mental resilience, fostering a supportive environment for overall well-being.
