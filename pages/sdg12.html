SDG #12: RESPONSIBLE CONSUMPTION AND PRODUCTION

Our planet’s resources are finite, and responsible consumption is crucial. This goal promotes practices that minimize waste and environmental impact, advocating for a circular economy that conserves resources and protects ecosystems. Urdaneta City University leads by example, implementing sustainable practices on campus such as recycling programs, composting, and offering sustainable dining options. The University organizes educational events on responsible consumption, inspiring students to adopt sustainable habits and advocate for environmental stewardship.
