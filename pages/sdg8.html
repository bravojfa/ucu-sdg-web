SDG #8: DECENT WORK AND ECONOMIC GROWTH

Economic growth should benefit everyone, not just a select few. This goal focuses on creating jobs, promoting entrepreneurship, and ensuring fair labor practices that lead to productive employment for all. Urdaneta City University supports career development by organizing job fairs, internships, and networking events that connect students with local businesses. The University also encourages entrepreneurship through workshops and competitions that inspire students to develop innovative ideas and startups, promoting economic growth in the community.

Bright future starts here.
