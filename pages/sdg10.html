SDG #10: REDUCED INEQUALITIES

Inequality hinders development and social cohesion. This goal addresses disparities in wealth and opportunities, promoting policies that ensure equal access to resources and opportunities for all. Urdaneta City University creates inclusive programs that support underrepresented groups, ensuring equal access to education and resources. The University facilitates dialogues on diversity, equity, and inclusion, raising awareness and fostering a more equitable campus environment.
