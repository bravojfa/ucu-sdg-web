SDG #7: AFFORDABLE AND CLEAN ENERGY

Access to energy is crucial for development. This goal advocates for the transition to renewable energy sources, ensuring that everyone can access sustainable energy solutions while increasing energy efficiency. Urdaneta City University has made significant investments in renewable energy sources for campus operations, including solar panels and energy-efficient buildings. The University also hosts educational events that raise awareness about the importance of clean energy and encourages students to engage in sustainability practices both on campus and in their communities.
