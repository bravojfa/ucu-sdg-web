SDG #6: CLEAN WATER AND SANITATION

Water is vital for life, yet billions of people lack access to safe drinking water and adequate sanitation. This goal aims to ensure universal access to these essential resources and promote sustainable water management practices. Urdaneta City University is committed to sustainability by implementing water conservation measures on campus, such as installing water-saving fixtures and promoting awareness about water use. The University collaborates with local organizations to enhance community access to clean water and sanitation facilities, contributing to improved public health.
