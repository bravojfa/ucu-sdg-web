SDG #11: SUSTAINABLE CITIES AND COMMUNITIES

Urbanization presents both challenges and opportunities. This goal focuses on developing sustainable urban spaces that provide access to safe housing, transportation, and public services, promoting resilience against climate change. Urdaneta City University engages students in local urban development projects, encouraging them to contribute ideas for improving community sustainability. Collaborating with local governments, UCU supports initiatives that promote green spaces, sustainable transportation, and community engagement in urban planning.
