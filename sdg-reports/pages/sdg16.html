SDG #16: PEACE, JUSTICE AND STRONG INSTITUTION

Peace and stability are fundamental for development. This goal aims to reduce violence, ensure the rule of law, and promote inclusive decision-making, fostering societies that uphold justice and human rights. Urdaneta City University fosters a culture of dialogue and respect through conflict resolution workshops and peacebuilding initiatives. The University encourages student participation in governance and decision-making processes, strengthening institutional accountability and promoting civic engagement.
