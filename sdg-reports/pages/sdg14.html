SDG #14: LIFE BELOW WATER

Our oceans are vital for planetary health. This goal seeks to protect marine ecosystems, reduce pollution, and promote sustainable fishing practices to ensure the health of our oceans for future generations. Urdaneta City University supports marine conservation initiatives by collaborating with organizations focused on ocean health. The University educates students about marine ecosystems through workshops and events, promoting awareness and advocacy for ocean preservation.

Bright future starts here.
