SDG #15: LIFE ON LAND

Biodiversity is essential for survival and ecosystem health. This goal emphasizes the need to preserve ecosystems, combat land degradation, and promote sustainable land management practices. Urdaneta City University engages in reforestation projects and supports biodiversity initiatives in the local area. The University incorporates environmental education into the curriculum, raising awareness about land conservation and sustainability among students.

Bright future starts here.
