SDG #2: ZERO HUNGER

Hunger is a critical issue affecting millions worldwide, particularly among vulnerable populations. This goal emphasizes the need for food security, improved nutrition, and sustainable agricultural practices. It calls for the reduction of food waste and the enhancement of food production systems to ensure that everyone has access to sufficient and nutritious food. Urdaneta City University actively collaborates with local food banks and community gardens, facilitating food drives and awareness campaigns about food insecurity. The University also hosts workshops focused on sustainable agricultural practices, encouraging students to engage in community gardening projects that promote local food production and reduce waste.
