SDG #1: NO POVERTY

Poverty is a multifaceted issue that goes beyond mere lack of income; it encompasses various deprivations in health, education, and living standards. This goal aims to eradicate extreme poverty globally, ensuring that everyone has access to essential resources such as nutritious food, clean water, healthcare, and shelter. Urdaneta City University is committed to reducing poverty by offering scholarships and financial assistance to underprivileged students, ensuring that financial barriers do not prevent talented individuals from pursuing higher education. Additionally, UCU engages in community outreach programs that provide educational workshops and resources to marginalized communities, empowering them with the knowledge and skills necessary for sustainable livelihoods.

Bright future starts here.
